The problem of potholes

Potholes are a widespread and persistent issue affecting roads worldwide, causing significant challenges for transportation departments, vehicle owners, and pedestrians. Their formation is due to a combination of environmental and mechanical factors, including weather conditions, traffic loads, and the quality of road construction materials.

What is pothole detection?

Pothole detection is an essential part of maintaining roads and ensuring safe driving conditions. It is a challenging task that requires accurate detection and monitoring of road conditions in real-time. With advancements in computer vision, the Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), the wide feeds of distributed cameras can be analyzed with deep learning models to inspect road conditions with AI.
